About Us

Founded in 2005

A market-leading boutique HR and Technology recruitment and executive search business.

We combine deep subject matter and market expertise, gained over 20 years of successful delivery in partnership with a global client base across multiple industry sectors.

UK & US offices

With offices in the UK and the USA, our consultants have deep, holistic knowledge of their sectors and the talent pools that lie within them.

Built on trust

Critically, we care deeply about the customers we serve and the relationships we build over the years, on a foundation of trust, confidentiality and delivery.

What We Do

We operate specialist teams across six core capabilities.

Each team partners with you as a genuine market specialist.

HR Recruitment

Covering all HR disciplines from Administrator to Chief People Officer — including Reward, Payroll, DEI, HR Operations, Global Mobility, Talent, and Transformation.

Executive HR Search

Specialising in the most senior HR leadership positions globally — discreet, research-led retained search for CHRO and CPO-level appointments.

Technology Recruitment

Through Oakleaf TCT (oakleaftct.com), specialising in Product, Data, Software Engineering, and Transformation roles.

Sector Focus

Financial Services, Professional Services, Commerce & Industry, Payroll, and Not-for-Profit & Public Sector — deep, holistic knowledge of every sector we serve.

US Corporate Functions

Our industry teams in the US support clients with mandates across Corporate Functions — Finance, Legal, Administration, Office Management, Procurement, Marketing and Operations.

International Support

International support across the USA, Ireland and EMEA — ensuring global organisations receive consistent, specialist service wherever they operate.
